Introduction

(G)I-DLE ((여자)아이들) is a K-Pop girl group consisting of 6 members, Miyeon, Minnie, Soojin, Soyeon, Yuqi, Shuhua of different nationalities under Cube Entertainment. They debuted in May 2nd 2018 with their debut mini album "I Am" with the title track "Latata".
